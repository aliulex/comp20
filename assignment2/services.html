About Us

Food: Delivered helps you find and order food from wherever you are. You type in an address and we’ll tell you the restaurants that deliver to that locale as well as showing you restaurants available for pickup near you. When you find what you're looking for, you can place your order online or by phone, free of charge. Oh, and we also give you access to reviews, coupons, special deals and a 24/7 customer care team that tracks each order and makes sure you get exactly what you want. We’re dedicated to connecting diners with the food they love from their favorite local restaurants and elevating food ordering through innovative restaurant technology, easy-to-use platforms and an improved delivery experience, generating new ways for people to earn, work and live along the way.
